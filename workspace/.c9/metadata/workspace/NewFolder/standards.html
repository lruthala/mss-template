Brand Identity and Corporate Standards

\n\t\t\t\t\t\t
\n\t\t\t\t\t
\nBrand Identity is a crucial aspect of any organization, and a study shows that 90% of the successful brands have a superior brand and marketing strategy than other organizations. Uniformity of Message, superior quality collateral and cleanliness of design all help in the process of creating a truly inspiring and impressive piece of work. Through our Corporate Standards and Guidelines packet we will be providing you with a huge array of templates, collateral and other brand related items which will help everyone in Team Miracle to consistently maintain the high standards that we set in terms of brand identity. Brand Identity can deteriorate very quickly, hence we request everyone to follow the guidelines strictly and contact marketing@miraclesoft.com if there are any questions (or) suggestions.\t\t\t\n\t\t
